Self Driving Car

based on Neural Networks

By Nihal Shah

This is a simulation of how AI would learn to drive a car. In this simulation you have to save the best car intelligence by pressing the 💾 button. Discard the saved best car intelligence by pressing 🗑️ button. You may need to save multiple times as the car would learn better the more time the simulation runs. On your right is the neural network visualization and on the left is the simulation.
